👁️ Overview

Welcome to Kafinder.com ("Kafinder", "we", "us", or "our"). We operate the website www.kafinder.com and provide free online finder tools and calculators designed for users in India.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using Kafinder.com, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

🔒 Our commitment: Kafinder does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We collect only what is necessary to operate and improve the website.

📊 Data We Collect

Kafinder is a free tool website. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our tools. However, certain data is collected automatically:

  • Log Data: When you visit our site, our servers may automatically record your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time of visit. This is standard practice for all websites.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how tools are used (e.g. which tool was searched, how many times) to help us improve the service. This data is never linked to you personally.
  • Contact Form Data: If you submit our contact form, we collect your name and email address solely to respond to your query. We do not store this data beyond what is necessary.

We do not collect: Aadhaar numbers, PAN, bank details, passwords, or any sensitive financial or health information.

🍪 Cookies

Kafinder uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your experience and to serve advertisements. Cookies we use include:

  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences (e.g. last searched tool) during a session.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (via Google Analytics, if enabled). All data is anonymised.
  • Advertising cookies: Set by Google AdSense to show relevant advertisements (see AdSense section below).

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain tools on our website. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones.

📢 Google AdSense & Advertising

Kafinder.com uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense is a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC.

  • Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites across the internet.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to Kafinder.com and other sites.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.
Google's privacy policy governing AdSense can be found at policies.google.com/privacy.

🔗 Third-Party Links

Kafinder has no control over the content or privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

👶 Children's Privacy

Kafinder.com is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@kafinder.com and we will delete such information promptly.

⚖️ Your Rights

As a user of Kafinder.com, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: You may request details of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request that we delete any personal information you have provided (e.g. via contact form).
  • Right to Opt-Out: You may opt out of personalised advertising via Google's Ad Settings.
  • Right to Complain: If you believe your data has been misused, you may raise a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in India.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@kafinder.com.
